The Challenge

The Healthcare Waste Crisis

Pharmacies lose millions in expired medicines while patients struggle to find essential medications

Unsold Bulk Medicines

Pharmacies purchase in bulk but can't sell all stock before expiry, leading to massive inventory waste.

₹2.5Cr lost annually per pharmacy

100% Expiry Losses

Once expired, medicines become worthless. No recovery, no refund - complete financial loss.

18-25% of stock expires unsold

No Digital Inventory

Manual ledgers and Excel sheets make it impossible to track expiry dates and stock levels efficiently.

70% pharmacies still manual

Patients Visit Multiple Stores

No way to check medicine availability online. Patients waste time visiting 3-5 pharmacies to find what they need.

Average 2.5 hours wasted
